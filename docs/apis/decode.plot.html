decode.plot.functions.plotspectrum(dataarray, shape, **kwargs)[source]¶

Plot a spectrum diagram.

Parameters:
  • dataarray (xarray.DataArray) – Dataarray with which the spectrum is plotted.
  • shape (str) – The shape of mask, ‘box’ or ‘circle’.
  • kwargs (optional) –
    When ‘box’ is specified as shape,
    xc: Center of x. yc: Center of y. width: Width. height: Height. xmin: Minimum of x. xmax: Maximum of x. ymin: Minimum of y. ymax: Maximum of y.
    When ‘circle’ is specified as shape,
    xc: Center of x. yc: Center of y. radius: Radius.

    xlim (list): Range of x. ylim (list): Range of y.

Notes

All kwargs should be specified as pixel coordinates.
